A group of researchers have conducted a study to see how people with different relationships make choices that affect each other. The study involved pairs of people who were either friends or enemies. Each pair of people played a game in which each player made a choice that resulted in each player getting a payout.

In the game, each player has two options, and the payouts are based on the options both players choose. Both players know the full table of payouts before choosing. One player chooses first, and the other chooses second. The first player does not know what the second player will choose. However, the second player does know the first player's choice.

On the following screens, you will see the choices that the pairs of people made. Your task will be to make a guess about whether the pairs are friends or enemies, based on the choices they made.
